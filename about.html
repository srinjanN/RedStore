Our Story

RedStore was founded in 2020 with a simple vision: make premium sports and lifestyle products accessible to everyone. What started as a small local store in Kolkata, West Bengal has grown into a thriving online marketplace.

Our founder, Mr. Srinjan Nandy, began by curating a small collection of athletic wear. Today, we carry hundreds of products across multiple categories — from T-shirts and shoes to watches and accessories.

We partner with trusted brands like Puma, Nike, HRX, and Fossil to bring you authentic products at competitive prices. Our team of dedicated professionals works every day to improve your shopping experience.
